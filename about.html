About Me

I'm a data science graduate student at Central Connecticut State University with a strong foundation in Python, statistics, and NLP. My academic background in physics shaped my analytical mindset, and my passion lies in uncovering insights from real-world user behavior and sentiment data.

My recent work includes a capstone project analyzing Steam reviews to model user engagement and predict playtime trends, as well as a sentiment and topic modeling project on IMDb movie reviews. I specialize in applying NLP techniques like lexicon scoring, topic modeling, and machine learning to messy, user-generated content.

I'm eager to apply my skills in a professional setting where I can contribute to meaningful data-driven decisions.
